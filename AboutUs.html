About Us

Charity Run 2024 is a community-driven event that combines sports, health, and charity. Our mission is to raise funds for environmental protection projects in Vietnam, while inspiring people to stay active and contribute to society.

Whether you are a runner, a volunteer, or a sponsor, you are welcome to join us in making a positive impact on both the environment and the community.
