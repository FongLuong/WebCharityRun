Rules and instructions for participants:

  • All participants must register in advance and comply with the event rules.
  • Participants must sign a liability agreement before joining the race.
  • Runners should arrive at least 30 minutes before the starting time for check-in and warm-up.
  • Appropriate sports attire and running shoes are required for safety.
  • No littering on the race course; please use the provided trash bins.
  • Follow the directions of the event staff and volunteers throughout the event.
