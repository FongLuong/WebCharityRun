Charity Run 2024 is a running event combining fundraising and raising community awareness about environmental protection. The event is scheduled to take place on on 11th October 2024 at Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center - SECC, Hochiminh City.

1. Event Objectives

  • Raise funds to support projects of the Vietnam Environmental Protection Organization.
  • Encourage a healthy lifestyle and promote sports spirit.
  • Create a community playground to spread the spirit of sharing.

2. Participants

  • Everyone aged 10 and above, regardless of gender or fitness level.
  • Professional athletes, sports enthusiasts, families, groups of friends, and organizations.

3. Running Distances

  • 3 km: For families and beginners.
  • 5 km: For those with good physical ability.
  • 10 km: For experienced runners.

4. Side Activities

  • Food area with healthy dishes and drinks.
  • Booths introducing eco-friendly products.
  • Games and entertainment programs for all ages.

5. Participant Benefits

  • Event T-shirt, bib number, and finisher medal.
  • Water and refreshment stations along the route.
  • Opportunities to win prizes and souvenirs.

6. Time & Venue

  • Date: October 15, 2024
  • Location: Thong Nhat Park, Hanoi
  • Opening Ceremony: 7:00 AM
