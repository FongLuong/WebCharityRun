Events:

  1. Art Performance & Opening Ceremony: The event will kick off with vibrant performances from famous singers, local bands, and dance groups. This opening is designed to create a lively, energetic atmosphere, inspiring all participants before they start their run.
  2. Running Events: Distances of 3km (Fun Run), 5km (Healthy Challenge), and 10km (Pro Runner) will be held. Each distance is tailored for different levels of fitness, encouraging everyone from beginners to experienced runners to join.
  3. Food & Refreshment Area: Enjoy a variety of healthy snacks, refreshing drinks, and local specialties. All proceeds from food sales will go directly to the Vietnam Environmental Protection Organization.
  4. Amusement & Family Zone: Fun games, face painting, photo booths, and mini sports activities will be available for both kids and adults, ensuring a fun day for the whole family.
